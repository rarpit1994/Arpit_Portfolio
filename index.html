About me

My passion for quantitative problem solving started at the Vellore Institute of Technology, India where I was fascinated to discover that mathematics and ant colony's pheromone-based communication together, gave birth to ACO, one of the most effective algorithms in computer science. I then graduated with a Masters in Applied Data Science , where I had dedicated myself to Data Science and was fortunate enough to be mentored by some of the greatest minds in this field.

I am now working as a Senior Data Scientist at Environment & Climate Change Canada. The case-based learning method is an amazing experience that has expanded my knowledge in business key areas and unique characteristics of various industries. Hailing from India, I am a English speaker, employing my technical and soft skills at leading organizations like Employment & Social Development Canada (ESDC) and Department of National Defense (DND) on data analytics and data science.

Having experiences ranging from India to Canada, I have always stayed consistent in loving to learn, whether that be about data science or about the secrets of healthy living. I found myself very interested in reading online articles about artificial intelligence and emerging technologies as well as books related to self-help, psychology and communication.
